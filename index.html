This is a game that tests the strength of you and your friends telepathic skills.
Beware, if it's not strong, it may break your years of friendship.
Players who disobey the following rules must take 1 shot. No exceptions.

What do you need:
  • One large communal device with a browser. e.g. Tablet or Laptop
  • Two smaller devices with a browser. e.g. Smartphones
  • 4+ players, the more the merrier.
  • A bottle of hard liquor.
  • Shot glasses.
  • Glass cup.
How to play:
  • Connect all devices to: 'https://ivanmsam.github.io'.
  • Enter a random 3 digit number in 'Game Number' in all 3 devices and click 'Start'.
  • This game consist of 25 blocks: 9 x RED, 8 x GREEN, 7 x YELLOW and 1 x BLACK.
  • The colours of each block is initially hidden.
  • The teams are split into two; the RED team and the GREEN team.
  • Each team elects a representative.
  • On their smaller individual devices, each representative click 'I'm the chosen one'.
  • The representative are given the power to see the colours of each block.
  • The representative's objective is to steer their team to select their coloured blocks.
  • The represenatives can ONLY say ONE word and ONE number each turn.
  • The represenatives must not show any expression during the game.
  • Commencing with the RED team, each representative takes turn to say ONE word and ONE number.
  • The team then needs to select a block on the large communcal device based on the word given by their representative.
  • The team's objective is to select their own coloured blocks.
  • If they select their team's colour, then they must take another turn up to the number their representative has stated.
  • If they select the opponent's block or a YELLOW block then their turn is over.
  • If they select a BLACK block then they automatically lose.
  • The first team to discover all their coloured blocks wins.
Penalties:
  • If your team selects a YELLOW block: 1 Shot
  • If your team selects an opponent's block: 1 Shot
  • If your team selects a BLACK block: Half cup
  • If your team loses: 2 Shots
